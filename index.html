FAQ

Common questions before we begin.

How do you approach weight loss?

Through sustainable nutrition, lifestyle habits, and realistic progress tracking rather than extreme restrictions.

Can you support healthy weight gain?

Yes — I create plans that focus on balanced nourishment, strength, and steady improvement.

What does the coaching process look like?

It begins with a consultation, a health assessment, and then a personalized plan with ongoing guidance.

How is BMI used?

BMI is a general screening tool that helps give context to health status and guide conversations around habits.

What kind of support is included?

Regular support, motivation, progress reviews, and practical adjustments as needed.
